Game Modules

Scrambled Word

Unscramble letters to form the correct word. This module helps improve spelling and vocabulary recognition.

  • Letter rearrangement challenges
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Hints and support options

Sentence Completion

Complete sentences by filling in the scrambled word. This module enhances grammar and contextual understanding.

  • Contextual vocabulary practice
  • Grammar structure reinforcement
  • Sentence formation skills

Sentence Builder

Build complete sentences using given words. This module develops sentence construction and syntax skills.

  • Syntax and structure practice
  • Word order understanding
  • Creative language use

About Lingua Bubble

Lingua Bubble is a completely free educational language learning application designed for users aged 18 and above. Our app offers three engaging game modules to help users improve their language skills in a fun and interactive way.

Unlike many other apps, Lingua Bubble does not require any user registration or login. We believe in providing a hassle-free learning experience without collecting any personal data.

Our backend provides content for each game in pages, and as you complete each item, the app shows them one by one. Once you finish all items in a set, the app automatically requests a new page of items from the backend, ensuring a continuous learning experience.

All game modules are available for free in all supported languages and proficiency levels, making quality language education accessible to everyone.

Privacy Policy

Lingua Bubble Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Lingua Bubble handles data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience. This policy explains what information we do and do not collect, and how we use any non-personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Device IDs
  • Location data
  • Any other personal information that could identify you

Data We Collect (Non-Personal)

Our application may connect to a backend service to retrieve content for our games (Scrambled Word, Sentence Completion, Sentence Builder). This data includes the game content itself (words, sentences, etc.) in multiple languages. We do not link this data to individual users or devices. The purpose of this connection is solely to provide you with the game content you need to play.

No Tracking or User Profiles

We do not track user behavior within the app and we do not build user profiles. Your activity is not logged or associated with any unique identifier. The data retrieval from our backend is a one-way process to deliver content to the app without storing any user-specific information on the server.

Backend Connection and Data Transfer

The only data exchange between your device and our backend service is the request and delivery of game content. This process does not involve the transfer of any personal or non-personal information about you, the user, to our servers.

Policy Updates
